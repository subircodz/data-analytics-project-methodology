# Purpose

The Analytical Thinking document captures the consultant's preliminary analytical ideas immediately after the Business Understanding phase.

At this stage, no data has been validated or analyzed.

The purpose is to transform the client's business problems into initial analytical hypotheses, identify possible metrics, anticipate required datasets, and prepare for the subsequent DAPM phases.

This document represents **thinking**, not **conclusions**.

---

# When to Use

Create this document after completing the Business Understanding phase.

Update it as stakeholder discussions and business requirements become clearer.

All ideas recorded here must be validated during later DAPM phases.
