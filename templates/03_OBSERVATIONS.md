# Purpose

The Observations document records objective findings collected throughout the project.

An observation should describe **what has been identified or verified** without explaining *why it occurred* or *what action should be taken*.

Observations serve as the evidence base for subsequent Business Insights and Recommendations.

---

# When to Use

Update this document throughout every DAPM phase whenever new verified information becomes available.

Observations may originate from:

- Client meetings
- Business process reviews
- Stakeholder discussions
- Data validation
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Business analysis
- Statistical findings

# Observation Guidelines

An observation should be:

- Objective
- Evidence-based
- Concise
- Verifiable
- Free from assumptions or recommendations

Avoid explaining causes or proposing solutions within this document.
