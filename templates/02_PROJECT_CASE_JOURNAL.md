# Purpose

The Project Case Journal serves as the chronological record of the entire analytics engagement.

It captures every significant interaction, discussion, decision, clarification, assumption, and milestone throughout the project lifecycle.

Unlike analytical reports, this journal records **how the project evolved**, providing complete traceability from the initial client meeting to final project delivery.

---

# When to Use

Begin this document immediately after the initial client meeting and maintain it throughout the project.

Create a new journal entry whenever:

- A client meeting is conducted
- A workshop or interview is completed
- A project milestone is achieved
- Business requirements change
- Important decisions are made
- New risks or assumptions are identified
