# Purpose

This report documents the exploratory analysis performed on the dataset after validation and data preparation.

The objective of EDA is to understand the dataset by identifying distributions, trends, relationships, and anomalies before drawing business conclusions.

EDA focuses on describing what the data shows rather than explaining why it happens.
